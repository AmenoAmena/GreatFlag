How to play GreatFlag

  1. Use the "Construct" button to build properties like houses, factories, hospitals, schools, universities, and medical schools.
  2. Each property has a construction cost in gold.
  3. Your objective is to construct 5 of each property to complete the game.
  4. You can also earn additional gold through special events or by guessing the correct number
    in the income section.
  5. Watch out for your gold balance. If it goes below zero, you lose the game.
  6. Keep an eye on the timer. The game will end when you complete all property
    constructions.
  7. Have fun and become the best president of Valoria!
  8. Dont forget that this is a speedrun game so try to make yor best time.
